Contest Rules

  • There are 3 teams: the Thunder team, the Fire team, and the Star team.
  • Every time you play games, you earn points for your team.
  • The competition lasts 3 weeks.
  • The team with the most points will win, and each member of that team will receive 30,000 Craft Coins
  • The team in second place will have each member earn 20,000 Craft Coins.
  • The team in third place will have each member earn 10,000 Craft Coins.
  • there are a exam of 4 questions
  • Based on your performance in the exam, we will receive various types of prizes
